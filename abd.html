Lebrons childhood

Lebron was born on December 30,1984 in Akron,Ohio. He was born to a single mother and his father was not around to support him. lebron and his mother lived in the projects and had very little money. Fortunately, Coach Frankie walker agreed to let lebron stay with him and his family to get away from the poverty and focus on school.
